Project Overview

What is Zoom AI Interview Prep?

Zoom AI Interview Prep is an AI-powered mock interview application designed to run directly inside a Zoom meeting. The experience helps users prepare for job interviews by analyzing their resume and job description, generating smart role-specific questions, and providing structured feedback after the interview.

The goal is to make interview preparation more accessible, realistic, and personalized by bringing AI coaching into a familiar video meeting environment.
